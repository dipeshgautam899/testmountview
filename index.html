Introduction to Hotel
Mount View Pokhara

Situated in the heart of the township of Pokhara, one of the most coveted destinations for the globetrotters, Hotel Mount View Pokhara is a newly established hotel facilitated with all kinds of hospitality services of international standard with local touch.This hotel is a close proximity of famous Phewa lake that serves as a mirror to the snow-capped majestic Fish Tail(Machhapuchhre) and Dhaulagiri Mountains.
